Owner account guidelines

To create an owner account, you must provide accurate and verifiable information about yourself and your properties. This includes real contact details and truthful boarding house data.

You’ll be responsible for managing listings, communicating with tenants, and updating your information regularly. Only one owner account is allowed per person.

Caution: False listings, duplicate accounts, or misuse of the platform may lead to limited access or permanent suspension of your account.
