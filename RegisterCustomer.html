Before you register

To create a customer account on HouseFinds, you must provide accurate and truthful information such as your full name, contact number, and a valid email address.

You’ll use this account to search, compare, and reserve boarding houses. Please avoid creating duplicate accounts.

Caution: Creating fake or multiple accounts may lead to restricted access or suspension from the platform.
