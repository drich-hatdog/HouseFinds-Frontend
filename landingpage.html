Your journey begins
with the right place to stay.

Connecting and helping students find boarding houses, and helping owners find reliable tenants — all in one platform with HouseFinds, where bookings and management are effortless.

Overview

Students of the University of Eastern Philippines often experience difficulties in searching for safe, affordable, and suitable boarding houses near the campus. Even with many options available, the increasing demand, different student preferences, and the absence of a centralized online platform make the process challenging for both students and boarding house owners. Studies also show that factors such as security, price, and location strongly influence a student’s choice of accommodation. To address these problems and provide a more convenient, organized, and accessible system, HouseFinds was developed as a web-based platform that helps students easily find and book boarding houses while also allowing owners to efficiently advertise their rooms.

Our Vision

To create a world where every student can easily find a safe, affordable, and convenient place to stay. We strive to make the search for boarding houses simple and stress-free. We envision a community where students feel supported and secure in their living choices. HouseFinds aims to become the go-to platform for student housing solutions.
